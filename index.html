▪ WORLDVIEW.TXT

$ cat worldview.txt

Behavior is data; data is people.

Every row in my datasets is someone's evening, habit, or bad week. Quantitative methods aren't a way to look past people — they're a way to take them seriously.

A convincing interface is a responsibility.

LLMs already sound caring. Whether they actually help — especially someone in crisis — is an empirical question, and one worth answering before scaling.

Fields over vignettes.

Real users don't behave like clinical scenarios. I build studies — bots, experiments, A/B tests — that meet people where they actually are: online, distracted, vulnerable.
